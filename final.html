Welcome to my Final Project's Page!

This is my final project for CSC 106, RPG Fight Game. In the game you have four attacks: fire a damaging move, sheild a blocking move, heal a healing move, and bless a buffing move. Mean while the enemy has only three moves, scream a stunning move, fire a damaging move, and poison a rare stunning and damaging move, that become more common the lower it's health. Will you be able to survive and defeat the monster or will you tast defeat?
